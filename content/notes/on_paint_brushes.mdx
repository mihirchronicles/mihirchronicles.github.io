Welcome to the world of paint brushes. 

Whether you're picking up a brush for the first time or looking to expand your toolkit, choosing the right brush can make all the difference between a frustrating experience and a flowing, expressive one.

Paint brushes aren't one-size-fits-all. They're precision tools, each designed with a specific purpose in mind. The shape of the bristles, the material they are made from, the length of the handle, even the way they hold paint - all of these factors shape what a brush can do and how it feels in your hand.

In this guide, you'll explore the most common brush types, discover what each one is best suited for, and learn how to match the right brush to your medium, whether that's oils, acrylics, watercolors, or gouache. Along the way, you'll pick up tips on care and maintenance so your brushes last for years.

Start building the brush knowledge that every confident artist needs. Let's get painting.
